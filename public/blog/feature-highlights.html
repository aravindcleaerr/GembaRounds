GembaRounds: 40+ Features That Transform Your Gemba Walks

Every capability of the AI-powered platform built to make shop floor leadership structured, data-driven, and effortless.

By Aravind & Team | April 2026 | 6 min read

GembaRounds isn't just a checklist app. It is a complete operational platform built around the PSS COACH methodology — bringing together AI intelligence, rich analytics, multi-role workflows, and seamless communication in one place. Here is every feature, organized by category.

COACH Framework Built In

Every walk in GembaRounds follows the five-step COACH framework designed by PS Satish Sir. The app guides the leader through each phase interactively — nothing is skipped, nothing is forgotten.

Fig 1: GembaRounds guides leaders through all 5 COACH steps with built-in checklists and prompts
  • Step-by-step walk wizard — each COACH phase unlocks sequentially
  • 15 observation area checklists — House Keeping, Safety, Discipline, Productivity, Wastes, Ambience, Environment, Material Handling, Data Recording, Standards, Machine Condition, Gauges & Fixture, Inventory, Review Meeting, Others
  • Appreciation log — positive observations tracked separately for culture metrics
  • In-walk action capture — raise action items mid-walk without breaking flow
  • Walk timer — tracks duration per phase for coaching feedback

AI-Powered Intelligence

GembaRounds brings six AI capabilities to the shop floor — moving beyond data capture into genuine insight generation.

Fig 2: Six AI capabilities that turn raw observations into actionable intelligence
Why AI Matters on the Shop Floor

On a busy floor, a leader captures 15–30 observations per walk. Without AI, categorising, prioritising, and summarising those takes 20 extra minutes. GembaRounds does it in under 5 seconds — so leaders focus on people, not paperwork.

Rich Analytics Dashboard

Data captured during walks flows automatically into a multi-chart analytics dashboard — giving managers, plant heads, and leadership instant visibility without any export or Excel work.

Fig 3: Dashboard charts — category doughnut, weekly bar, closure trend line, and spaghetti floor heat map
  • Category doughnut chart — split of observations across all 15 areas
  • Walk frequency bar chart — daily / weekly / monthly cadence at a glance
  • Issue closure trend line — raised vs resolved over time
  • Spaghetti diagram (floor heat map) — visualise which zones have the most issues
  • Appreciation vs issue ratio — culture health metric per leader
  • Area score card — rolling score for each of the 15 observation areas
  • Leader leaderboard — walk count and quality ranking across the team
  • Drill-down filters — by area, leader, date range, severity, and status

Multi-Role Access

GembaRounds enforces a five-tier role hierarchy — ensuring the right people see the right data and can take the right actions.

Fig 4: Five-role hierarchy — from Operator to Admin, each with scoped permissions
  • Invite by email — role assigned at invite time, changeable by Admin
  • Area-scoped data — Supervisors see only their zone; Leaders see all
  • Action ownership — each action item has an assignee, due date, and status
  • Role-based dashboard views — each role sees a tailored home screen
  • Audit trail — every change is logged with user and timestamp

Communication Built In

Observations and reports don't sit in a silo — GembaRounds pushes information directly to the people who need it, through the channels they already use.

Fig 5: WhatsApp, Email, and PDF — three zero-friction sharing channels
  • WhatsApp share — formatted text + key stats sent to any group in one tap
  • Email digest — daily or weekly summaries to configured recipients
  • PDF walk report — includes observations, photos, action items, and scores
  • In-app notifications — real-time alerts when an action is assigned or overdue
  • Action escalation — overdue items automatically nudge the next-level role

Works Everywhere

GembaRounds is designed to work anywhere on the shop floor — on any device, in any language, even with poor connectivity.

Fig 6: PWA, mobile, desktop, dark mode, and bilingual support — every context covered
  • Progressive Web App (PWA) — install directly from the browser, works offline
  • Responsive mobile UI — large tap targets, swipe navigation, portrait and landscape
  • Full desktop experience — wide analytics panels, keyboard-accessible
  • Dark mode — auto-detects system preference, manual toggle available
  • Hindi language — complete UI translation for operators and supervisors
  • Photo capture — in-walk camera access, annotate and attach to observations
  • Offline queue — observations saved locally sync when connectivity returns
Built for Indian Manufacturing

GembaRounds is designed with Indian shop floors in mind — Hindi language support, WhatsApp-first sharing, and a UI that works on affordable Android handsets with variable connectivity.

Everything Else — Feature by Feature

Walk Management

  • Schedule recurring walks by role, area, and frequency
  • Walk templates — pre-configured checklists per area type
  • Multi-area walks — cover several zones in a single session
  • Co-walk mode — two leaders walk together, one record
  • Walk history — searchable, filterable log of every past walk

Action Item Tracking

  • Create actions mid-walk or post-walk
  • Priority levels — Critical, High, Medium, Low
  • Due date with automatic reminder escalation
  • Evidence upload — photo or note when closing an action
  • Reopen actions if closure evidence is insufficient
  • Bulk action assignment to a team or area

Reports & Exports

  • Walk report PDF — branded, shareable, print-ready
  • Monthly trend report — emailed automatically
  • Area scorecard export — CSV for ERP integration
  • Leader performance report — walk count, action closure rate
  • Custom date range filters on all exports

Settings & Configuration

  • Organisation profile — name, logo, plant details
  • Custom observation areas — add or rename the 15 defaults
  • Notification preferences — per-user, per-event
  • Branding on PDF and email reports
  • Data retention policy — configurable archive period
