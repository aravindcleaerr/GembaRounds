# GembaRounds - Pricing Model

## Tier Structure

### Free (Beta)
- **Price:** Free forever
- **Users:** Up to 10
- **Walks:** Unlimited
- **Storage:** 512 MB (MongoDB Atlas free)
- **Features:** All core features
- **Support:** Community/email
- **Best for:** Individual managers, small teams trying it out

### Starter
- **Price:** Rs 2,999/month (~$36/year billed annually)
- **Users:** Up to 25
- **Walks:** Unlimited
- **Storage:** 5 GB
- **Features:** All features + CSV export + email sharing
- **Support:** Email (48hr response)
- **Best for:** Single factory, small SME

### Professional
- **Price:** Rs 7,999/month (~$96/year billed annually)
- **Users:** Up to 100
- **Walks:** Unlimited
- **Storage:** 25 GB
- **Features:** All features + multi-factory + admin dashboard + audit trail + priority support
- **Support:** Email + WhatsApp (24hr response)
- **Best for:** Multi-factory operations, medium enterprises

### Enterprise
- **Price:** Custom (contact sales)
- **Users:** Unlimited
- **Storage:** Unlimited
- **Features:** Everything + on-premise deployment + custom integrations + SSO + dedicated support
- **Support:** Dedicated account manager
- **Best for:** Large enterprises, hospital chains, multi-location retail

## Revenue Projections

### Year 1 (Conservative)
- 5 Free users (lead gen)
- 10 Starter @ Rs 2,999/mo = Rs 29,990/mo
- 3 Professional @ Rs 7,999/mo = Rs 23,997/mo
- **Monthly Revenue: ~Rs 54,000 (~$650)**
- **Annual Revenue: ~Rs 6.5 lakh (~$7,800)**

### Year 2 (Growth)
- 20 Free users
- 30 Starter @ Rs 2,999/mo = Rs 89,970/mo
- 10 Professional @ Rs 7,999/mo = Rs 79,990/mo
- 2 Enterprise @ Rs 25,000/mo = Rs 50,000/mo
- **Monthly Revenue: ~Rs 2.2 lakh (~$2,600)**
- **Annual Revenue: ~Rs 26 lakh (~$31,000)**
