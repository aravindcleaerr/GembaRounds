1. Project Overview

GembaRounds is a full-stack digital Gemba Walk platform that transforms traditional pen-and-paper Gemba walks into an AI-powered, data-driven system. It is built specifically on PSS (PS Satish) methodology for operational excellence in manufacturing environments.

"Go to Gemba. Connect with operating people. Appreciate good things. Check standards. Find improvements."

What Problem Does It Solve?

  • Paper-based Gemba walks lack traceability and follow-through
  • Observations are lost or forgotten after the walk
  • No systematic way to track recurring issues
  • Difficult to measure improvement over time
  • Employee involvement is limited to managers

3. Demo Walkthrough (Step-by-Step)

Follow these steps to experience the complete GembaRounds workflow:

1

Login as Manager

Open gembarounds.vercel.app. Login with rajesh@factory.com / pass123. You'll see the Home page with welcome message, PSS areas, how-to guide, suggestion box, and leaderboard.

2

Start a New Walk

Click "New Walk" button. Enter title (e.g., "Morning Floor Walk"), your name, frequency (Daily), and click "Begin Walk". You'll be taken to the Walk Interface.

3

Record a Positive Observation

Click "Good Thing" toggle (green). Title: "Clean workstation at CNC Bay 3". Description: "Excellent 5S maintained, tools organized on shadow board". Select Area: "House Keeping (5S)", Category: "Other", Location: "CNC Bay 3", Person Met: "Sunil". Click "Save Observation".

4

Record a Negative Observation with AI

Click "Improvement" toggle (red). Title: "Safety guard missing". Description: "Press machine 4 safety guard not properly latched, hazard for operator". Click "AI Categorize" - it auto-fills Category: Safety, Area: Safety, Type: Negative. Add Person Tagged: "Maintenance Head". Save.

5

Use Voice Dictation

Click the purple "Voice" button. Speak your observation (e.g., "Operators are waiting fifteen minutes between batches because material is not arriving from previous station"). The text appears automatically. Click "AI Categorize" to auto-fill fields. Save.

6

Capture Photo Evidence

Click the camera upload zone. Take a photo or select from gallery. The photo preview appears. Multiple photos/videos can be attached. Save the observation with visual evidence.

7

Check IoT Floor Monitor

Scroll down to see the IoT Floor Monitor showing live machine status. Running/Idle/Error counts, average OEE, and alerts for high temperature or vibration. Click "Expand" to see individual machine details.

8

Complete the 10-Questions Framework

Scroll to "10-Questions Framework". Answer each Gemba Moonwalker question (takt time, pacemaker, flow, etc.). Navigate with number buttons. Click "Save" when done. Responses are saved as an observation.

9

Complete the Walk

Click the red "Complete Walk" button at the top. The walk status changes to "Completed". A "Summary" button appears - click it to open the printable walk summary report in a new tab.

10

View Reports & Charts

Navigate to Reports tab. See stat cards, doughnut charts (waste categories, positive vs negative), horizontal bar chart (by PSS area), and week-over-week trend analysis. Click "Export CSV" to download all data.

11

Add Weekly Review

In Reports, find the completed walk. Click "Review". Enter reviewer name ("PS Satish Sir") and notes ("Safety guard critical - fix by Monday. Good 5S at CNC Bay 3."). Save. The review appears below the walk.

12

Track Actions

Navigate to Actions tab. See all tagged action items with Open/In Progress/Closed status. Click the status dropdown to update. Add notes for follow-up. Filter by clicking stat cards.

13

Search History & Find Patterns

Navigate to History tab. Search by keyword, filter by area/category/type/location. The system automatically detects repeated issues and highlights them in red.

14

Toggle Dark Mode

Click the moon icon in the top navigation bar. The entire app switches to a beautiful dark theme. Click the sun icon to switch back. Preference is remembered.

15

Test as Operator

Logout. Login as sunil@factory.com / pass123 (Operator role). You can view reports, submit suggestions via the Employee Suggestion Box (earns Kaizen points), and see the leaderboard. Operators cannot start walks - that's a manager privilege.

4. Complete Feature Guide

1 Authentication & Roles

JWT-based register/login with three roles: Admin, Manager, Operator. Managers start walks and review. Operators submit suggestions. Guest access available for demos.

2 Gemba Walk Management

Start walks with title, walker name, and frequency (daily/weekly/bimonthly/monthly/ad-hoc). Full lifecycle: Scheduled → In Progress → Completed → Reviewed.

3 Observation Recording (15 PSS Areas)

Record both positive (appreciate) and negative (improve) observations across all 15 PSS areas. Capture location, person met, person tagged for action, with photo/video and voice dictation.

4 AI Auto-Categorization

One-click AI analysis of description text. Auto-detects waste category (Muda/Mura/Muri/Safety/Quality), observation area from 15 PSS areas, and positive vs negative sentiment using keyword NLP.

5 10-Questions Framework (Gemba Moonwalker)

Interactive assessment: business problem, process ownership, takt time, pacemaker, flow, capability. Progress tracking with circular navigation. Saves as observation.

6 IoT Floor Monitor

Live machine data for 8 machines: OEE, temperature, vibration, cycle time, parts produced, defect rate. Real-time alerts for critical conditions. Auto-refreshes every 10 seconds.

7 Reports & Dashboard

6 stat cards, doughnut charts (waste categories, positive/negative ratio), horizontal bar chart (by area), week-over-week trend analysis (stacked bar + line chart). CSV export.

8 Walk Summary Report

Full HTML printable report with walk metadata, stats, good things table, improvements table, action items, review notes. Print or save as PDF directly from browser.

9 Scheduling & Recurring Walks

Schedule future walks with date picker. Set up auto-recurring walks (daily/weekly/bimonthly/monthly) with cron-based scheduling. View upcoming walks dashboard.

10 Action Tracking

All "Person Tagged" observations become action items. Track status: Open → In Progress → Closed. Filter by status with clickable stat cards. Add follow-up notes.

11 Search & History

Full-text search across all observations. Filter by area, category, type, location. Automatic Repeated Issues Detection flags recurring problems at same locations.

12 Notifications & Alerts

Overdue actions (7+ days), pending reviews, overdue scheduled walks, hotspot areas (5+ observations). Severity-sorted: Critical → Warning → Info.

13 Employee Involvement

Employee Suggestion Box on home page. Operators submit observations directly without a walk. Each suggestion earns 10 Kaizen points. No manager approval needed.

14 Gamification & Leaderboard

Kaizen Points: 5 pts for being met during walk, 10 pts for submitting suggestions. Leaderboard with rank, contributions, total points. Top 3 highlighted with medals.

15 Progressive Web App (PWA)

Install on phone like a native app. Service worker for offline caching. Mobile-optimized with bottom navigation bar. Responsive design for all screen sizes.
