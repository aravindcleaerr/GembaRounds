# GembaRounds - Go-To-Market Plan

## Product Summary
GembaRounds is an AI-powered Gemba Walk platform that digitizes shop floor observations using the PSS COACH methodology. Built for Indian manufacturing SMEs.

## Target Market

### Primary: Indian Manufacturing SMEs
- 50-500 employee companies
- CNC, machining, assembly, fabrication shops
- Currently doing Gemba walks on paper or not at all

### Secondary Markets
- Hospitals (ward rounds, safety audits)
- Warehouses & logistics centers
- Construction/EPC project sites
- Retail chains (store audits)

## Go-To-Market Phases

### Phase 1: Beta (Current - Q2 2026)
- **Free tier** for early adopters
- Target: 10-20 manufacturing units
- Goal: Validate product-market fit, gather feedback
- Channel: PSS network, LinkedIn, manufacturing forums

### Phase 2: Launch (Q3 2026)
- **Freemium model** with paid tiers
- Target: 50-100 companies
- Channel: Industry events, MSME associations, consultant referrals
- Partnership with lean/kaizen consultants

### Phase 3: Scale (Q4 2026 - 2027)
- **SaaS product** with onboarding support
- Target: 500+ companies
- Channel: Digital marketing, channel partners, resellers
- Playstore/App Store listing (PWA wrapper)

## Market Introduction Steps

1. **PSS Network Launch** - PSS Sir introduces to his consulting clients
2. **Case Study** - Document results from 3-5 beta users
3. **Industry Events** - Demo at CII, ACMA, MSME events
4. **LinkedIn Campaign** - Manufacturing-focused content marketing
5. **Consultant Partnerships** - Partner with lean/TPM consultants who recommend the tool
6. **MSME Schemes** - Apply under Digital India, MSME Udyam programs for subsidized adoption
7. **Referral Program** - Existing users get credits for referrals

## Success Metrics
- Monthly Active Users (MAU)
- Walks per user per week
- Observation-to-action closure rate
- Net Promoter Score (NPS)
- Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)

- **Phase 1 (Beta):** Zero (free tier hosting)
